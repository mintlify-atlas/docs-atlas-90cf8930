## Navigation Modes

The app offers two distinct navigation modes:

### Destination-Based Navigation

Search for a specific destination and follow the calculated route:
- **Search any location**: Cities, addresses, businesses, landmarks
- **Optimized routing**: Fastest route calculated automatically
- **Turn-by-turn guidance**: Visual route on the map
- **Distance preview**: See total kilometers before starting
- **Time estimate**: Know how long your trip will take
- **Auto-complete**: Automatic mileage update upon arrival

### Free Tracking Mode

Track your mileage without a specific destination:
- **No destination required**: Just hit "Iniciar Recorrido Libre"
- **Real-time tracking**: Follow your path as you drive
- **Precise mileage**: Every meter is tracked and recorded
- **Manual completion**: End tracking whenever you want
- **Automatic updates**: Mileage saves to your vehicle on completion

<Info>
Free Tracking Mode is perfect for road trips, test drives, or when you're driving locally without a fixed destination.
</Info>

## How to Navigate

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Navigation">
    From your vehicle dashboard, tap the "Rutas" button in the Tools section. The app requests GPS permissions and loads the map centered on your current location.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Search for Destination">
    Type your destination in the search bar at the top. Results appear as you type, showing:
    - Full address
    - City and region
    - Distance from your location
    - Location pin icon
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Your Destination">
    Tap any search result to select it. The destination appears on the map with a **red flag marker**, and the app automatically traces the optimal route.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Route Information">
    The bottom panel displays:
    - **Distance**: Total kilometers for the route
    - **Time**: Estimated travel time in minutes
    - **Destination name**: Full address shown at the top
    
    The route appears as a **blue line** on the map.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Navigation">
    Tap the green "Iniciar ruta" button. The navigation begins:
    - Your position is marked with a **blue navigation arrow**
    - GPS tracking activates with high accuracy
    - The map follows your movement in real-time
    - The route changes from blue to **green** as you travel it
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Reach Your Destination">
    When you arrive (within 50 meters of the destination), navigation automatically completes:
    - A completion dialog appears
    - Your vehicle's mileage updates in the database
    - You see total kilometers traveled
    - New mileage is displayed
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Free Tracking Mode

For trips without a specific destination:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start Free Tracking">
    From the idle map view, tap the blue "Iniciar Recorrido Libre" button at the bottom.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Drive Anywhere">
    The app tracks your position continuously:
    - **Green path** shows where you've traveled
    - **Real-time distance** displays in the top bar
    - Map follows your movement automatically
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Finish Tracking">
    Tap the red "Finalizar recorrido" button when done. Confirm in the dialog, and your mileage updates instantly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Use Free Tracking Mode for daily commutes, errands, or any time you want to track mileage without planning a specific route.
</Tip>

## Route Calculation

When you select a destination, the app:

1. **Queries OSRM**: Sends your current location and destination to the routing engine
2. **Receives route geometry**: Gets coordinates for the optimal path
3. **Parses GeoJSON**: Converts routing data to map points
4. **Calculates metrics**: Determines total distance and estimated time
5. **Draws on map**: Renders the blue route line
6. **Fits bounds**: Automatically zooms to show entire route

<Note>
OSRM routing considers real road networks, one-way streets, and typical speeds for each road type to provide accurate routes.
</Note>

## Mileage Tracking

The app uses precise distance calculation:

```dart
const distance = Distance();
final segmentMeters = distance.as(LengthUnit.Meter, lastPos, newPos);
final segmentKm = segmentMeters / 1000.0;
```

- **High-precision GPS**: 5-meter filter for position updates
- **Haversine formula**: Calculates exact distance between GPS points
- **Cumulative tracking**: Adds each segment to total distance
- **Sub-kilometer accuracy**: Tracks down to individual meters

### Automatic Mileage Updates

When navigation completes:

1. App retrieves current mileage from database
2. Rounds traveled distance to nearest kilometer
3. Adds traveled distance to current mileage
4. Updates the `vehiculos` table in Supabase
5. Shows completion dialog with new mileage

<Warning>
Ensure GPS and location permissions are enabled for accurate tracking. Navigation won't work without location access.
</Warning>

## Manual Completion

You can end navigation anytime by tapping "Finalizar recorrido":

- A confirmation dialog shows your current distance
- Tap "Finalizar" to save mileage and return to dashboard
- Tap "Cancelar" to continue navigating

## Error Handling

The app gracefully handles common issues:

- **No GPS signal**: Prompts to enable location services
- **Permission denied**: Shows alert to grant location access
- **Routing failure**: Displays error message and allows retry
- **No network**: Offline maps continue working; routing requires internet

<Tip>
For best results, ensure you have a stable internet connection before starting navigation. The map tiles and route calculation require network access.
</Tip>

## Search Capabilities

The Nominatim geocoding service supports:

- **Addresses**: Street names, house numbers, postal codes
- **Cities and regions**: Navigate to entire cities
- **Businesses**: Restaurants, hotels, gas stations
- **Landmarks**: Parks, monuments, tourist attractions

## Privacy & Data

- **No third-party tracking**: All navigation uses open services
- **Local processing**: GPS data stays on your device
- **Minimal uploads**: Only final mileage updates go to the server
- **No route history**: Past routes are not stored
- **Anonymous usage**: OpenStreetMap and OSRM don't track users
