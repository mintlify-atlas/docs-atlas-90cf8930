## How to Add a Vehicle

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Vehicle Type">
    Select whether you're adding a car or motorcycle using the vertical tabs on the left side of the screen.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Brand">
    Browse through the horizontal brand selector displaying logos of supported manufacturers. Each brand has its own distinctive color theme:
    - **Yamaha**: Blue
    - **Suzuki**: Red
    - **BMW**: Dark Gray
    - **Kawasaki**: Green
    - **Toyota**: Red
    - **Chevrolet**: Yellow
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Pick Your Model">
    Use the image carousel to browse through available models for your selected brand. Navigate using the left and right arrow buttons to find your exact vehicle.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Vehicle Details">
    Fill in the following information:
    - **Current Mileage**: How many kilometers your vehicle has traveled
    - **Year**: The model year of your vehicle
    - **Nickname**: A custom name for easy identification (e.g., "Demon", "My Ride")
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create Your Vehicle">
    Tap the "Create Vehicle" button to save your vehicle to the database. You'll be automatically taken to your vehicle dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Vehicle Dashboard

Once registered, your vehicle appears on the main dashboard with:

- **Vehicle image** from the catalog
- **Brand logo** displayed in the corner
- **Model and nickname** prominently shown
- **Current mileage** tracked in real-time
- Quick access to all maintenance and document features

## Technical Details

Vehicle data is stored in the Supabase `vehiculos` table with the following fields:

- `user_id`: Links vehicle to your account
- `marca`: Vehicle brand
- `modelo`: Specific model name
- `apodo`: Your custom nickname
- `kms`: Current mileage
- `image_path`: Path to the vehicle image asset

<Note>
All vehicle information is securely stored in the cloud and syncs across your devices when you're logged in.
</Note>
