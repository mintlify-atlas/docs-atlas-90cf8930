# Adding Vehicles

My Auto Guide allows you to register both cars and motorcycles in your account. This guide will walk you through the process of adding a new vehicle.

## Overview

The vehicle registration screen provides a visual catalog organized by brand, allowing you to browse and select your exact model before entering vehicle details.

<Steps>

### Step 1: Choose Vehicle Type

When you first access the Add Vehicle screen, you'll see two tabs on the left side:
- **Carro** (Car)
- **Moto** (Motorcycle)

Tap the appropriate tab to select the type of vehicle you want to register.

### Step 2: Select Your Brand

Scroll through the horizontal brand selector at the bottom of the screen. Available brands include:

**For Motorcycles:**
- YAMAHA (blue)
- SUZUKI (red)
- BMW (black/gray)
- KAWASAKI (green)
- HONDA (red)
- DUCATI (orange-red)
- KTM (orange)
- BAJAJ (blue)
- HERO (black)
- AKT (dark blue)

**For Cars:**
- TOYOTA (red)
- MAZDA (black)
- CHEVROLET (yellow)

Each brand logo is displayed with its signature color, making it easy to identify.

### Step 3: Browse and Select Your Model

Once you select a brand:
1. The main area displays a carousel of available models with high-quality images
2. Use the left/right chevron buttons to navigate through models
3. The model name appears below the image as you browse
4. Tap directly on a model image to select it

<Tip>
The carousel shows actual product images for each model, helping you confirm you're selecting the right vehicle.
</Tip>

### Step 4: Enter Vehicle Details

After selecting your model, fill in the following fields:

**Kilometraje (Mileage)**
- Enter the current odometer reading
- Example: `6500` for motorcycles, `25000` for cars
- This is used to track maintenance schedules

**Modelo (Year)**
- Enter the model year of your vehicle
- Example: `2024` or `2022`
- Format: 4-digit year

**Apodo (Nickname)**
- Give your vehicle a friendly name
- Example: "Demon" for a motorcycle, "Mi Nave" for a car
- This appears throughout the app to help you identify your vehicle

<Warning>
Make sure to enter accurate mileage information, as this is critical for calculating maintenance reminders.
</Warning>

### Step 5: Create Your Vehicle

Once all fields are complete:
1. Tap the **"Crear Vehiculo"** (Create Vehicle) button
2. The app will save your vehicle to the database
3. You'll be automatically navigated to the main dashboard

<Note>
Your vehicle information is securely stored and linked to your user account. You can add multiple vehicles and switch between them.
</Note>

</Steps>

## Tips for Accurate Registration

### Verify Your Selection
Double-check that the model image and name match your actual vehicle before proceeding. The visual catalog includes specific variants (e.g., "PULSAR NS 200 FI ABS" vs "PULSAR NS 200 UG").

### Accurate Mileage Entry
- Check your odometer directly before entering the value
- For motorcycles, typical range is 0-50,000 km
- For cars, typical range is 0-200,000+ km
- Round to the nearest kilometer

### Choosing a Nickname
Pick a memorable name that helps you quickly identify your vehicle, especially if you plan to register multiple vehicles.

## What Happens Next?

After creating your vehicle, you'll be taken to the main dashboard where you can:
- View your vehicle's details and image
- Set up maintenance schedules
- Upload vehicle documents
- Track trips and mileage
- Access safety guides and tools

## Switching Vehicle Types

If you accidentally select the wrong vehicle type (Car vs Moto), simply tap the other tab on the left side of the screen. Your progress will reset, allowing you to start fresh with the correct vehicle type.

## Common Issues

**Model not listed?**
The app includes an extensive catalog of popular models. If your specific model isn't listed, choose the closest variant from the same brand and year.

**Can't see brand logos?**
Ensure you have a stable internet connection. Brand logos load from the app's assets and should appear immediately.

**Save button not working?**
Make sure all required fields are filled in:
- Mileage (can be 0 for new vehicles)
- Model year
- Vehicle model is selected from the carousel

The app will show an error message if any required information is missing.
