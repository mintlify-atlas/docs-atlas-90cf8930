# Manuals and Videos

My Auto Guide includes an educational library of safety guides and video tutorials to help you maintain your vehicle and handle roadside situations with confidence.

## Overview

The Guides section provides two types of resources:

1. **Interactive Safety Guides** - Step-by-step checklists for common situations
2. **Video Tutorials** - YouTube videos showing maintenance procedures

Both resources are accessible offline-first (guides) or require internet (videos), ensuring you have help when you need it.

## Accessing the Guides

<Steps>

### Step 1: Open the Guides Section

From your vehicle dashboard:
1. Scroll to the **"Herramientas"** (Tools) section
2. Tap the **"Guía"** button
3. The Safety Guides screen opens

### Step 2: Choose Your Guide or Video

The screen displays:
- **Four guide cards** at the top (interactive checklists)
- **Video Tutorials section** below (YouTube videos)
- Each option is clearly labeled with an icon and title

</Steps>

## Interactive Safety Guides

The app includes four comprehensive safety guides:

### 1. Minor Accident (Accidente Leve)

**When to use:** Small collisions, fender benders, minor damage, no injuries

**Icon:** Blue car crash symbol

**Steps included:**
1. Stay calm and verify everyone is okay
2. Move to a safe location to avoid another accident
3. Assess damage and check for minor injuries
4. Exchange information with the other driver (name, plate, insurance)
5. Document the scene (take photos)
6. Notify your insurance company

<Tip>
This guide includes a built-in camera feature for documenting the accident scene directly in the app.
</Tip>

### 2. Serious Accident (Accidente Grave)

**When to use:** Major collisions, injuries, significant damage, blocked roadway

**Icon:** Red warning symbol

**Steps included:**
1. Stay calm and assess your safety
2. Call emergency services immediately
3. Avoid moving injured people unless absolutely necessary
4. Place warning triangles and flares to signal the scene
5. Document the scene (take photos)
6. Inform your insurance and cooperate with authorities

<Warning>
Serious accidents require immediate emergency response. Call 123 (Colombia emergency number) before using the app guide.
</Warning>

### 3. Rainy Weather Driving (Clima Lluvioso)

**When to use:** Driving in rain, wet roads, reduced visibility conditions

**Icon:** Teal cloud symbol

**Steps included:**
1. Reduce speed and increase following distance
2. Turn on low-beam headlights for visibility
3. Avoid sudden braking or sharp turns
4. Do not cross flooded areas
5. Keep windshield clean and defroster on

<Note>
This guide is designed to be reviewed before driving in rainy conditions, not while actively driving.
</Note>

### 4. Long Trip Preparation (Viaje Largo)

**When to use:** Before road trips, long-distance travel, highway journeys

**Icon:** Orange route symbol

**Steps included:**
1. Verify vehicle condition before departure (lights, brakes, tires)
2. Ensure you're well-rested before the trip
3. Plan stops every 2 hours to stretch and rest
4. Bring water, first aid kit, and basic tools
5. Always wear seatbelt and avoid distractions
6. Monitor weather and road conditions

## Using Interactive Guides

<Steps>

### Step 1: Select a Guide

1. Tap the guide card that matches your situation
2. The guide opens showing all steps in sequence
3. Each step has a circular checkbox on the left

### Step 2: Work Through Steps

As you complete each step:
1. **Tap the circle** next to the step
2. It fills with the guide's color and shows a checkmark
3. The step is marked complete
4. Progress is automatically saved
5. Move to the next step

<Tip>
You don't have to complete steps in order. Check off steps as you accomplish them in whatever order makes sense for your situation.
</Tip>

### Step 3: Document with Photos (When Applicable)

Some steps (like "Document the scene") include a camera feature:

1. Look for the **"Tomar Foto"** (Take Photo) button below the step
2. Tap to open your camera
3. Take clear photos of:
   - Vehicle damage from multiple angles
   - License plates of all vehicles involved
   - Overall scene showing vehicle positions
   - Any relevant road signs or conditions
   - Injuries if applicable (with permission)

4. Photos appear below the button as thumbnails
5. Each photo has two icons:
   - **Download icon** - Save to your device's gallery
   - **Delete icon** (red) - Remove the photo

### Step 4: Save Photos to Gallery

To keep photos permanently:
1. Tap the **download icon** on any photo
2. The app saves it to your device's photo gallery
3. A confirmation message appears
4. Photos remain in the app until you delete them

### Step 5: Review Progress Anytime

- Checked steps remain checked when you return to the guide
- Photos persist between sessions
- Progress is saved locally using SharedPreferences
- Each guide maintains its own independent progress

</Steps>

<Note>
Guide progress and photos are stored locally on your device. They won't sync to the cloud or transfer to other devices.
</Note>

<Steps>

1. Select the tutorial you want to watch
2. Tap anywhere on the card

### Step 2: Opens in YouTube App

1. The video opens in your device's YouTube app (if installed)
2. Or opens in your default web browser
3. Video begins at the start
4. Use standard YouTube controls:

   - Seek/scrub timeline

   - Enable captions if available

3. Your guide progress is preserved

</Steps>

<Note>
Videos open externally in YouTube to provide the best viewing experience and to avoid data usage within the app. YouTube Premium subscribers can watch ad-free.
</Note>

## Best Practices

### Before an Emergency

- **Review guides** in advance so you know what's available
- **Practice** using the photo feature so you're comfortable
- **Watch videos** when learning maintenance procedures
- **Bookmark** relevant videos for quick reference

### During an Emergency

- **Stay safe first** - don't use your phone while driving
- **Pull over** completely before opening guides
- **Work through steps** methodically
- **Document thoroughly** - more photos are better than too few
- **Save photos** to gallery immediately (in case app closes)

### For Maintenance

- **Watch videos first** to understand the full procedure
- **Pause and replay** sections as needed
- **Gather tools** before starting work
- **Take your time** - rushing leads to mistakes
- **Update your maintenance dates** in the app after service

## Managing Guide Data

### Resetting Progress

Currently, there's no built-in reset button. To start fresh:
- Manually uncheck all steps by tapping them again
- Or reinstall the app (this clears all guide data)

### Clearing Photos

To remove photos from a guide:
1. Open the guide
2. Scroll to the photo step
3. Tap the **red delete icon** on each photo
4. Confirm deletion
5. Photos are permanently removed from app storage

<Warning>
Deleting photos from the guide does NOT delete them from your device gallery if you previously saved them there.
</Warning>

### Storage Considerations

- Each photo is stored at device camera resolution
- Photos are compressed to save space
- Large numbers of photos (50+) may slow down the guide
- Delete old photos periodically to free up space

## Troubleshooting

### Guide Won't Open

- **Restart the app** and try again
- **Check device storage** - very low storage can cause issues
- **Update the app** if an update is available

### Photos Not Saving

- **Grant camera permission** in device settings
- **Grant storage permission** for saving to gallery
- **Check storage space** - photos need available space
- **Try taking photo again** if it doesn't appear

### Video Won't Play

- **Check internet connection** - videos require data
- **Install YouTube app** for best experience
- **Try opening in browser** if YouTube app fails
- **Verify video URL** hasn't been removed by creator

### Progress Not Saving

- **Don't force-close** the app immediately after checking steps
- **Allow a moment** for auto-save to complete
- **Check device storage** - very low space can prevent saves
- **Restart app** to load latest saved state

### Can't Download Photo to Gallery

- **Grant storage permission** in device settings
- **Check gallery app** is working properly
- **Verify storage space** is available
- **Try again** after restarting the app

## Privacy and Data

### Local Storage

- All guide progress stored locally on your device
- Photos stored in app-private storage
- No cloud sync for guide data
- Uninstalling the app removes all guide data

### Saved Gallery Photos

- Photos you save to gallery remain on device
- Subject to your device's photo backup settings (Google Photos, iCloud, etc.)
- Your responsibility to manage/delete from gallery
- May sync to cloud services depending on your settings

<Note>
Guide photos are separate from vehicle documents you upload elsewhere in the app. Accident photos from guides are stored locally, not in your cloud vehicle records.
</Note>
