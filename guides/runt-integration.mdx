# RUNT Integration

My Auto Guide integrates directly with Colombia's RUNT (Registro Único Nacional de Tránsito) to help you check your vehicle's official records and automatically extract important document dates.

## What is RUNT?

The **Registro Único Nacional de Tránsito (RUNT)** is Colombia's national vehicle and driver registry. It maintains official records for:

- Vehicle ownership and registration
- SOAT (mandatory vehicle insurance) status
- Revisión Técnico-Mecánica (technical inspection) certificates
- Vehicle history and transactions
- Traffic violations and fines

<Note>
RUNT is the authoritative source for vehicle information in Colombia. Data shown in RUNT is legally binding and used by authorities.
</Note>

## Why Use RUNT Integration?

The RUNT integration in My Auto Guide provides several benefits:

### Automatic Data Extraction
- Pulls SOAT issue and expiration dates automatically
- Extracts Tecnomecánica dates without manual entry
- Reduces errors from manual date entry
- Saves time when configuring maintenance reminders

### Official Verification
- Confirms your SOAT is valid and registered
- Verifies your technical inspection is current
- Checks against the national database
- Provides peace of mind for legal compliance

### Document Syncing
- Updates your vehicle record with official dates
- Automatically sets maintenance reminders
- Keeps your app data synchronized with RUNT
- Eliminates need to manually track expiration dates

<Warning>
You'll need your vehicle's license plate number (placa) and your national ID number (cédula) to use this feature. Keep these handy before starting.
</Warning>

## Accessing RUNT from the App

<Steps>

### Step 1: Open RUNT Consultation

From your vehicle dashboard:
1. Scroll to the **"Herramientas"** (Tools) section
2. Locate the **"Consultar RUNT"** button
3. Tap to open the RUNT consultation screen

### Step 2: RUNT Website Loads

The app opens an embedded web browser showing:
- The official RUNT Consulta Ciudadana website
- URL: `https://www.runt.com.co/consultaCiudadana/`
- A loading indicator while the page loads
- The full RUNT consultation interface

<Note>
The RUNT interface is embedded directly in the app using a secure WebView. You're accessing the real RUNT website, not a simulation.
</Note>

### Step 3: Auto-filled Form

Once the page loads:
- The **Placa** (license plate) field is automatically filled with your vehicle's plate number
- The **Número de Documento** (ID number) field is auto-filled with your cédula
- Both fields should appear populated within 1-2 seconds

<Tip>
If the fields don't auto-fill immediately, wait a few seconds for the page to fully load. The auto-fill script runs automatically.
</Tip>

### Step 4: Submit the Consultation

1. Verify the auto-filled information is correct
2. Tap the **"Consultar"** (Consult) button on the RUNT website
3. Wait for RUNT to retrieve your vehicle's information (5-10 seconds)
4. The results page appears with your vehicle details

### Step 5: Extract Dates Automatically

After the results appear:
1. Scroll to the bottom of the screen
2. Tap the blue **"Consultar Fechas SOAT y Tecno"** button
3. The app automatically:
   - Scans the RUNT page for SOAT table
   - Extracts issue and expiration dates
   - Scans for Tecnomecánica table
   - Extracts technical inspection dates
   - This process takes 5-20 seconds

<Note>
The app uses JavaScript to intelligently read the RUNT page tables. It looks for specific keywords like "FECHA EXPEDICIÓN" and "FECHA VENCIMIENTO" to find the correct dates.
</Note>

### Step 6: Review Extracted Data

Once extraction completes:
- A green success notification appears at the top
- A card at the bottom shows the extracted information:
  - SOAT issued: [date]
  - SOAT expires: [date]
  - Tecnomecánica issued: [date]
  - Tecnomecánica expires: [date]

- All dates are in the format displayed by RUNT

### Step 7: Data Automatically Saved

The app automatically:
1. Saves the extracted dates to your vehicle record in the database
2. Updates your SOAT and Tecnomecánica status
3. Recalculates maintenance indicators
4. Schedules expiration reminders

<Tip>
Return to your dashboard to see the updated SOAT and Tecnomecánica indicators with the official dates from RUNT.
</Tip>

</Steps>

## Understanding the Extraction Process

The RUNT integration uses advanced web scraping:

### How It Works

1. **Page Analysis** - The app analyzes the HTML structure of the RUNT results page
2. **Table Detection** - Identifies tables containing "PÓLIZA SOAT" and "CERTIFICADO DE REVISIÓN TÉCNICO-MECÁNICA"
3. **Row Scanning** - Reads each row in the relevant tables
4. **Date Extraction** - Finds cells containing "FECHA EXPEDICIÓN" and "FECHA VENCIMIENTO"
5. **Data Parsing** - Extracts the date values from adjacent cells
6. **Validation** - Ensures dates are in valid format
7. **Database Update** - Saves dates to your vehicle record

### Retry Logic

The extraction is resilient:
- **Up to 10 retry attempts** if data isn't immediately available
- **2-second intervals** between retries
- **Waits for RUNT** to fully load tables
- **Handles slow connections** gracefully

<Note>
If you see "searching..." for more than 20 seconds, the RUNT page may be loading slowly or have a different format than expected. Try refreshing the page.
</Note>

## Troubleshooting RUNT Integration

### RUNT Website Won't Load

**Possible causes:**
- No internet connection
- RUNT website is down (check www.runt.com.co directly)
- Firewall or network blocking access

**Solutions:**
- Check your internet connection
- Try switching between Wi-Fi and mobile data
- Wait a few minutes if RUNT servers are overloaded
- Use the refresh button to retry

### Auto-fill Not Working

**Possible causes:**
- Page hasn't fully loaded
- RUNT changed their form field names
- JavaScript blocked

**Solutions:**
- Wait 5-10 seconds after page load
- Manually type your plate and cédula if auto-fill fails
- Ensure JavaScript is enabled (it should be by default)

### "No Data Found" or Empty Results

**Possible causes:**
- Vehicle not registered in RUNT (unlikely)
- Incorrect plate number
- Incorrect cédula
- Vehicle registered under different owner

**Solutions:**
- Double-check your plate number is correct
- Verify the cédula matches the registered owner
- Ensure there are no spaces or typos in the input
- Contact RUNT directly if issue persists

### Date Extraction Returns Empty Dates

**Possible causes:**
- RUNT page hasn't fully loaded
- Your SOAT or Tecnomecánica is expired/not registered
- RUNT page layout changed

**Solutions:**
- Wait for the full RUNT results to display before extracting
- Scroll through the page to ensure tables are visible
- Try the extraction button again after 10 seconds
- Manually note dates and enter them in Maintenance Configuration

### Extracted Dates Look Wrong

**Possible causes:**
- Date format confusion (day/month vs month/day)
- RUNT displaying dates in unexpected format
- Extraction parsed wrong cell

**Solutions:**
- Verify dates against what's shown on screen
- Manually correct dates in Maintenance Configuration if needed
- Check the original document (physical SOAT/Tecno) to confirm

## Privacy and Security

### What Data is Accessed?

The RUNT integration:
- **Reads** public vehicle information available through RUNT's citizen portal
- **Extracts** only date fields for SOAT and Tecnomecánica
- **Saves** these dates to your private vehicle record
- **Does NOT** store your cédula or transmit it anywhere except to RUNT

### Data Security

- All communication with RUNT uses **HTTPS encryption**
- Your cédula is used only for the RUNT consultation
- Extracted dates are stored securely in your private account
- No personal data is shared with third parties

<Warning>
Never share your RUNT login credentials if prompted. The citizen consultation portal should NOT require a login - only plate and cédula. If asked for a password, you may be on a phishing site.
</Warning>

## Best Practices

### When to Use RUNT Integration

- **After renewing** SOAT or Tecnomecánica
- **When first** setting up a vehicle in the app
- **Quarterly** to verify your documents are current
- **Before long trips** to ensure legal compliance

### Keeping Data Current

- Run RUNT consultation after each document renewal
- The app doesn't auto-sync - you must manually trigger consultation
- Combine with uploading document images for complete records
- Set calendar reminders to check RUNT monthly

### Alternative Data Entry

If RUNT integration isn't working:
- Manually enter dates in [Maintenance Configuration](/guides/maintenance-configuration)
- Reference your physical SOAT and Tecnomecánica documents
- The app works fully without RUNT - it's a convenience feature

## Limitations

Be aware of these limitations:

- **Colombia-specific** - RUNT only covers Colombian vehicles
- **Requires internet** - Cannot work offline
- **RUNT uptime** - Dependent on government website availability
- **Layout changes** - RUNT website updates may break extraction temporarily
- **Public data only** - Can only access data available to all citizens

<Note>
The RUNT integration is a convenience feature. You can fully use My Auto Guide without it by manually entering your maintenance dates.
</Note>
