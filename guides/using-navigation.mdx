# Using Navigation

My Auto Guide includes a powerful GPS navigation system that helps you reach your destination while automatically tracking your vehicle's mileage. Built with 100% free and open-source technologies.

## Navigation Features

The navigation system provides:

- **Real-time GPS tracking** with high accuracy
- **Route calculation** using OSRM (Open Source Routing Machine)
- **Interactive maps** powered by OpenStreetMap
- **Automatic mileage tracking** that updates your vehicle's odometer
- **Free tracking mode** for drives without a specific destination
- **Turn-by-turn guidance** with visual route display

<Note>
All navigation features are completely free. No subscriptions, no API limits, no hidden costs.
</Note>

## Understanding the Navigation Screen

### Map Display

The main screen shows:
- **Your current location** - Blue circle with navigation icon
- **Destination marker** - Red circle with flag icon (when route is active)
- **Route line** - Blue line showing the optimal path
- **Traveled path** - Green line showing where you've driven
- **Map tiles** - High-quality CartoDB Voyager street maps

### Status Bar

The top bar displays:
- **Back button** - Return to dashboard
- **Search field** - Find destinations (when idle)
- **Navigation status** - Shows mode and distance traveled (when active)

### Information Panel

The bottom panel shows:
- **Destination name** and address
- **Total route distance** in kilometers
- **Estimated travel time** in minutes
- **Distance traveled** during navigation (green chip)

## Navigation Modes

The app operates in different states:

1. **Idle** - Ready to search for a destination
2. **Route Ready** - Route calculated, ready to start
3. **Navigating** - Actively following a route to a destination
4. **Free Tracking** - Recording mileage without a destination
5. **Completed** - Trip finished, mileage updated

## Navigating to a Destination

<Steps>

### Step 1: Access Navigation

From your vehicle dashboard:
1. Scroll to the **"Herramientas"** (Tools) section
2. Tap **"Rutas"**
3. Wait for GPS to acquire your location
4. The map centers on your current position

<Tip>
For best GPS accuracy, use navigation outdoors with a clear view of the sky. Allow a few seconds for GPS to lock onto your position.
</Tip>

### Step 2: Search for Your Destination

1. Tap the search bar at the top
2. Type your destination:
   - Street address
   - Business name
   - Landmark or point of interest
   - City or neighborhood

3. Search results appear as you type:
   - Up to 5 matching locations
   - Full addresses displayed
   - Location pin icon for each result

4. Tap the correct result from the list

<Note>
The search is powered by Nominatim, which searches OpenStreetMap's extensive worldwide database. Be as specific as possible for best results.
</Note>

### Step 3: Review the Route

After selecting a destination:
1. The app calculates the optimal route (3-5 seconds)
2. The route appears as a blue line on the map
3. The map automatically zooms to show the full route
4. The bottom panel displays:
   - **Destination name**
   - **Total distance** (e.g., "12.5 km")
   - **Estimated time** (e.g., "23 min")

5. Review the route on the map
6. Use pinch gestures to zoom in/out if needed

### Step 4: Start Navigation

When ready to depart:
1. Tap the green **"Iniciar ruta"** (Start Route) button
2. Navigation begins immediately:
   - Your position updates in real-time
   - The traveled path turns green
   - Distance traveled appears in the status bar
   - The map follows your movement

<Warning>
Do NOT use your phone while driving. Mount your device securely and set up navigation before starting your trip. Pull over safely if you need to interact with the app.
</Warning>

### Step 5: Follow the Route

While navigating:
- **Blue dot** shows your current position
- **Blue line** shows remaining route
- **Green line** shows path you've traveled
- **Distance counter** updates continuously
- Map auto-centers on your position

The app tracks:
- Exact distance traveled (accurate to 5 meters)
- Time elapsed
- Progress toward destination

### Step 6: Arrive at Destination

When you reach your destination:

**Automatic Completion:**
- When you're within 50 meters of the destination
- The app automatically completes the trip
- A completion dialog appears

**Manual Completion:**
- Tap the red **"Finalizar recorrido"** (Finish Trip) button
- Confirm in the dialog that appears
- Useful if you park nearby but not exactly at the pin

### Step 7: Trip Summary

A completion dialog shows:
- **Distance traveled** for this trip (e.g., "12.3 km")
- **New total mileage** for your vehicle
- Confirmation that the odometer has been updated

Tap **"Aceptar"** to:
- Return to your vehicle dashboard
- See the updated mileage immediately
- Trip data is permanently saved

</Steps>

## Free Tracking Mode

For trips without a specific destination, use Free Tracking Mode to automatically record mileage.

<Steps>

### When to Use Free Tracking

- Exploring or joy riding
- Running multiple errands without a set route
- Testing the bike/car
- Any trip where you don't need navigation

### Starting Free Tracking

1. Open the Navigation screen
2. Don't search for a destination
3. Tap the **"Iniciar Recorrido Libre"** (Start Free Tracking) button
4. The app begins recording immediately:
   - Status bar shows "Modo Libre" (Free Mode)
   - No destination marker appears
   - Distance counter starts from 0.0 km
   - Your path is traced in green

### During Free Tracking

- The map follows your position
- Distance accumulates automatically
- No route guidance is provided
- You're free to go anywhere
- The trip continues until you manually end it

### Ending Free Tracking

1. Tap the red **"Finalizar recorrido"** button whenever you're ready
2. Confirm the total distance
3. Mileage is added to your vehicle
4. Return to dashboard

</Steps>

<Tip>
Free Tracking is perfect for motorcycle enthusiasts who enjoy exploring without a fixed destination. Your mileage still gets recorded accurately!
</Tip>

## Automatic Mileage Updates

One of the most valuable features is automatic odometer tracking:

### How It Works

1. **GPS tracks** your exact position every 5 meters
2. **Distance calculation** uses high-precision geodesic formulas
3. **Running total** accumulates throughout your trip
4. **Database update** happens when you complete the trip
5. **Vehicle record** is updated with new total mileage

### Accuracy

The system is highly accurate:
- GPS accuracy: ±5-10 meters under good conditions
- Distance filter: 5 meters (prevents GPS drift from inflating distance)
- Calculation method: Haversine formula (accounts for Earth's curvature)
- Typical accuracy: Within 1-2% of actual distance

<Note>
Mileage tracking accuracy depends on GPS signal quality. In tunnels, dense urban areas, or under heavy tree cover, accuracy may decrease temporarily.
</Note>

### Viewing Updated Mileage

After any navigation session:
1. Return to your dashboard
2. Check the **"Kilometraje"** display in the vehicle info card
3. The value reflects the trip you just completed
4. Maintenance indicators automatically recalculate based on new mileage

When not actively navigating:
- A floating action button appears in the bottom-right
- Tap it to center the map on your current position
- Useful if you've panned away from your location

### Zoom Controls

Use standard gestures:

## Canceling a Route

Before starting navigation, you can cancel:

1. Review the calculated route
2. Tap the **"Cancelar"** button in the bottom panel
3. The route clears and you return to idle mode
4. Search for a different destination or close navigation

<Note>
Once you start navigating, there's no "cancel" - only "finish." Finishing the trip updates your mileage even if you didn't reach the destination.
</Note>

## Best Practices

### Before You Start

- **Mount your device** securely where you can see it
- **Set your destination** completely before moving
- **Check the route** makes sense for your needs
- **Ensure good GPS signal** (outdoors, clear sky view)
- **Charge your device** or connect to power (GPS drains battery)

### While Navigating

- **Don't interact** with the phone while moving
- **Glance only** at the screen for route confirmation
- **Pull over** if you need to check details or change settings
- **Follow traffic laws** over the suggested route
- **Stay alert** to real-world conditions the map doesn't show

### After Your Trip

- **Verify mileage** was updated correctly
- **Check maintenance indicators** for any new warnings
- **Note any route issues** (road closures, etc.) you encountered

## Troubleshooting

### GPS Won't Acquire Position

- **Enable location services** in device settings
- **Grant location permission** to My Auto Guide
- **Move outdoors** if you're inside
- **Wait 30-60 seconds** for initial GPS lock
- **Restart the app** if position doesn't appear

### Route Calculation Fails

- **Check internet connection** (required for routing)
- **Try a different search term** for your destination
- **Verify destination exists** and is reachable by road
- **Search for nearby address** if exact location doesn't work

### Map Not Loading

- **Check internet connection** (maps tiles require data)
- **Wait a few seconds** for tiles to download
- **Zoom out** to load lower-resolution tiles first
- **Restart the app** if tiles remain blank

### Mileage Not Updating

- **Verify internet connection** when finishing trip
- **Check you completed** the trip properly (tapped finish button)
- **Refresh dashboard** by pulling down or reopening
- **Review trip history** to see if trip was recorded

### Inaccurate Distance Tracking

- **Poor GPS conditions:**
  - Indoor/underground parking
  - Dense urban canyons
  - Heavy tree cover
  - Tunnels

- **Improve accuracy by:**
  - Pausing tracking until GPS improves
  - Keeping device with clear sky view
  - Avoiding pocket storage while tracking

### Battery Draining Quickly

- GPS is power-intensive, this is normal
- **Connect to vehicle power** during navigation
- **Reduce screen brightness**
- **Close other apps** running in background
- **Consider** bringing a power bank for longer trips
