## What is My Auto Guide?

My Auto Guide is your all-in-one solution for vehicle management, combining essential features into a single, easy-to-use mobile app:

- **Vehicle Management** - Register and track multiple cars and motorcycles
- **Maintenance Tracking** - Never miss an oil change, filter replacement, or inspection
- **Document Storage** - Keep all your vehicle documents secure and accessible
- **GPS Navigation** - Get turn-by-turn directions with automatic mileage tracking
- **Smart Notifications** - Receive timely reminders for maintenance and document renewals
- **RUNT Integration** - Access official vehicle information directly from the app

## How It Works

My Auto Guide uses a powerful technology stack to deliver a seamless experience:

- **Flutter** - Cross-platform mobile framework for smooth performance on Android and iOS
- **Supabase** - Secure backend for authentication, database, and file storage
- **OpenStreetMap** - Free, open-source maps for GPS navigation
- **Local Notifications** - Timely reminders that work even when you're offline

## Built for You

Whether you're a car enthusiast, a daily commuter, or a motorcycle rider, My Auto Guide adapts to your needs:

- Support for multiple vehicle brands (Toyota, Mazda, Chevrolet, Yamaha, KTM, Bajaj, and more)
- Customizable maintenance schedules based on your driving habits
- Easy document uploads from your camera or gallery
- Real-time GPS navigation with automatic mileage updates
- Offline-capable notifications so you never miss a reminder

<Note>
My Auto Guide is designed to work on both Android and iOS devices. Make sure you have the required permissions enabled for the best experience.
</Note>
