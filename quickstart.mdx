# Quickstart Guide

This guide will walk you through setting up My Auto Guide and adding your first vehicle in just a few minutes.

## What You'll Accomplish

By the end of this quickstart, you'll have:

- Created your My Auto Guide account
- Added your first vehicle (car or motorcycle)
- Configured maintenance schedules
- Uploaded your first vehicle document
- Set up notifications for upcoming maintenance

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- An Android (5.0+) or iOS (12.0+) device
- Internet connection for initial setup
- Your vehicle information handy (license plate, mileage, etc.)
- At least one vehicle document (SOAT, insurance, etc.)

<Note>
My Auto Guide requires certain permissions (GPS, Camera, Storage) to function properly. You'll be prompted to grant these during setup.
</Note>

## Step 1: Install the App

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download My Auto Guide">
    Download the app from your device's app store:
    
    - **Android**: Google Play Store
    - **iOS**: Apple App Store
    
    Or build from source if you're a developer. See the [Installation Guide](/installation) for detailed instructions.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Grant Required Permissions">
    When you first open the app, you'll be asked to grant permissions:
    
    - **Location**: Required for GPS navigation
    - **Camera**: Required to photograph documents and vehicles
    - **Storage**: Required to save and manage documents
    
    Tap **Allow** for each permission to enable full functionality.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 2: Create Your Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Launch the app">
    Open My Auto Guide on your device. You'll see the login screen with the app logo and sign-in options.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign up with email">
    1. Tap **Sign Up** or **Create Account**
    2. Enter your email address and create a strong password
    3. Tap **Register** or **Create Account**
    4. Check your email for a verification link
    5. Click the verification link to activate your account
    6. Return to the app and log in with your credentials
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Your data is securely stored with Supabase, a trusted backend platform. Your password is encrypted and never stored in plain text.
</Info>

## Step 3: Add Your First Vehicle

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start adding a vehicle">
    After logging in, you'll see the main dashboard. Tap the **Add Vehicle** button or the **+** icon to begin.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose vehicle type">
    Select whether you're adding a:
    - **Motorcycle** (Yamaha, Suzuki, BMW, Kawasaki, Honda, Ducati, KTM, Bajaj, Hero, AKT)
    - **Car** (Toyota, Mazda, Chevrolet, and more)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select brand and model">
    1. Choose your vehicle's brand from the list
    2. Browse the visual model catalog
    3. Select your specific model
    4. If your exact model isn't listed, choose the closest match
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter vehicle details">
    Fill in the required information:
    
    - **License Plate**: Your vehicle's registration number
    - **Current Mileage**: Current odometer reading in kilometers
    - **Year**: Model year of your vehicle
    - **Nickname** (optional): A friendly name like "My Blue Bike" or "Family Car"
    
    <Tip>
    Enter accurate mileage - this is crucial for maintenance tracking and will be automatically updated when you use GPS navigation.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save your vehicle">
    Review your information and tap **Save** or **Add Vehicle**. Your vehicle will now appear on the dashboard!
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 4: Configure Maintenance Schedules

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open maintenance settings">
    From your vehicle's dashboard, tap the **Maintenance** or **Settings** button to configure when you last performed each service.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set last maintenance dates">
    For each maintenance type, tap to select the date you last performed it:
    
    - **Chain Lubrication**: When you last lubricated the chain (motorcycles only, every 15 days)
    - **Air Filter**: Last air filter change (every 90 days)
    - **Oil Change**: Last oil and filter change (every 25 days)
    - **SOAT**: Last SOAT renewal date (annual)
    - **Technomechanical**: Last inspection date (annual)
    
    <Info>
    Don't worry if you don't remember the exact dates - use your best estimate. The app will calculate remaining time from these dates.
    </Info>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save configuration">
    Tap **Save** to update your maintenance schedule. The app will immediately calculate and display:
    
    - Percentage of time remaining for each service
    - Color-coded indicators (green, yellow, or red)
    - Automatic notifications when services are due
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
The app uses these maintenance intervals:
- Chain lubrication: 15 days
- Air filter: 90 days  
- Oil change: 25 days
- SOAT: 365 days
- Technomechanical: 365 days
</Note>

## Step 5: Upload Your First Document

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to documents">
    On your vehicle dashboard, scroll to the **Documents** section. You'll see cards for:
    - SOAT
    - Technomechanical Inspection
    - Insurance
    - Property Card
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a document to upload">
    Tap on any document card (e.g., **SOAT**) to begin uploading.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select upload method">
    Choose how to add your document:
    
    - **Take Photo**: Use your camera to photograph the physical document
    - **Choose from Gallery**: Select an existing photo or PDF from your device
    
    <Tip>
    For best results when photographing documents, ensure good lighting and hold the camera steady for a clear image.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload and verify">
    After selecting your file:
    1. The app uploads it securely to cloud storage
    2. A checkmark appears on the document card
    3. Tap the card again to view your uploaded document
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Supported formats: PDF, JPG, JPEG, PNG, WEBP, HEIC, GIF. Maximum recommended file size: 10MB.
</Info>
